OUR MISSION

We don't build hope, we build futures

National Commission for Human Development is a non-profit organization with the mission to transform lives by improving access to basic education and healthcare in the country’s poorest communities. NCHD is the leading agency fighting illiteracy in 124 districts of Pakistan and helping people to find routes out of ignorance.

Our mission is to support the government by filling the implementation gaps and improve public sector delivery mechanisms to achieve the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) and “Education For All” with focus on:

Goal 1: Achieve Universal Primary Education
Goal 2: Promote Gender Equality and empower women
Goal 3: Reduce Child Mortality
Goal 4: Improve Maternal Health
